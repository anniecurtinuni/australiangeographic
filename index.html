Venus is the second planet in our solar system. It is also the hottest one, with the average temperature being a sweltering 480 degrees celsius! Venus is quite unusual because it rotates backwards on it's axis. It can be called Earth's "toxic twin" because they are similar in size and structure, but Venus has a very toxic atmosphere - the clouds would smell like rotten eggs.
